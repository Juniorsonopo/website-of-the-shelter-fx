What is Forex?

Forex, or foreign exchange, is the largest financial market in the world where currencies are traded. It operates 24 hours a day, allowing traders to buy, sell, exchange, and speculate on currency prices. The forex market is essential for international trade and investment, providing opportunities for profit through currency fluctuations.

Why Trade Forex?

  • High liquidity and accessibility.
  • Opportunities to trade 24/5.
  • Potential for substantial returns.
  • Diversification of investment portfolio.
